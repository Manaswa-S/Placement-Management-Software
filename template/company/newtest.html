  • For insights, analytics and automatic results, grant access of the form to the admin:
    Steps:
    1) Share
    2) Collaborators
    3) Add this email : {{.NewTestGoogleFormsCollaboratorEmail}}
    4) Set Role as Editor
    5) Send
  • Copy the public respondents link of the Google Form and paste it in the input field below for students.
  • Make sure to Publish and Close the form at the right time.
